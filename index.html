Location

Though Bokaro city has an airport, it is only used by the private aircraft of SAIL. The other airport located near Bokaro city is the Birsa Munda Airport in Ranchi. The Birsa Munda Airport connects Ranchi to other major cities such as Mumbai, Bengaluru, Kolkata, etc. by leading airlines such as GoAir, IndiGo and Indian Airlines. If you are travelling from other parts of the country, such as Hyderabad, Pune, Amritsar, etc. you should take a flight to Kolkata and then board a bus or a cab to Bokaro. All leading airlines such as Jet Airways, Indian Airlines, Spice Jet, IndiGo, connect Kolkata to other cities in India.

Nearest Airport: The nearest airport is in Ranchi, the Birsa Munda Airport. It is located at a distance of 113 km from Bokaro.
